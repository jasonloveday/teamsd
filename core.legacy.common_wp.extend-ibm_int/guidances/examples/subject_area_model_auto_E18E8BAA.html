Subject Areas

 

The chart above depicts twelve subject areas. Subject Areas are groupings of logically related information used primarily for planning purposes. The consolidated information requirements, from 145 specific Business Unit questions, map to one of these subject areas.
In most cases, the contents of each subject area adequately describe it. Some areas may need clarification. Engineering design and specifications are in Product, implementation of engineering change is in Plant. Contracted supplier costs are in Product, as a product profitability component, purchase orders are in Purchasing. Production forecasting is in Product, along with other demand information. Production planning is in Plant. Customer is a subject area, while VIN contains customers who were owners of a vehicle. Although warranty claims are in VIN, damage claims are in Logistics.
