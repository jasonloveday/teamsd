The FGDC Ground Transportation Subcommittee is sponsoring the development of a conceptual data model standard for identifying road segments as unique geo-spatial features that are independent of any cartographic or analytic network representation. These road segments will form the basis for maintenance of NSDI framework road data (through transactions and other means), and for establishing relationships between road segments and attribute data.

Subject Area Modeling
ER Models, which detail all or part of an organization, are typically large. An application will often cover some 60 or so Entities while a whole Organization could cover some 500-1000. Models of this size and complexity, while necessary for implementation, are highly indigestible at the overview or planning levels and do not facilitate distribution of the applications.
They must therefore be reduced to a smaller number of components and these have been given the name Subject Areas.
