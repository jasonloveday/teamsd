Main Description

It is:

1) A free format, multi-section report combining textual, tabular and simple diagrammatic representations of business strategy insights
2) An agreed Business Strategy Summary report that can easily be integrated with other deliverables from the Insight Phase

It is generally used to:

1) Define completeness of the client's strategic business planning documents
2) Perform required internal (e.g., customer, product market, SWOT, financial and cost price analysis) and external analysis (e.g., industry, market and competitive analysis)
3) Validate results with senior management.
