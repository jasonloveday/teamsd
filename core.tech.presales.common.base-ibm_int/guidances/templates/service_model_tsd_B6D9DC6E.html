Main Description
This template is an abbreviated version of the Service Model templates associated with the Service Model artifact and its sub-artifacts. In a typical pre-sale scenario, only a few key new or existing services are defined and less detail is required. This template includes the typically required subset of content extracted from templates for the three sub-artifacts of the Service Model. In many cases, this template should be sufficient for pre-sale activity. For unusually complex situations requiring higher levels of elaboration, the Technical Solution Architect may wish to refer to the Service Model sub-artifacts and their associated templates. In such cases, also consider requesting assistance of a Subject Matter Expert.
