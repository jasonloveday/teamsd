Guideline: Pain Chain for Team Solution Design
This guideline provides direction in a pre-sale context regarding a useful tool that may be used to understand a client's environment and objectives.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Understand Business Environment and Objectives
Main Description

A useful tool is the "pain chain" which is a graphical representation of the interdependency of business problems (pains) within a client's organization. It may be unique to a particular opportunity or common to many. This tool illustrates how to capture issues and concerns at executive levels and map the to issues and concerns at other levels.
