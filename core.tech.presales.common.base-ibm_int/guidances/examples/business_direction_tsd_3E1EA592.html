Business Direction Work Product Example

Vision 

  • Increase customer satisfaction by decreasing problem resolution time/streamline customer support and reducing complexity of business processes.

Business Goals

  • Move from regional to a national bank status and gain customers and market share in the process. 

Business Objectives

  • Implement use of 3rd party Customer Support Reps (CSRs) within 1 year.

Tactics

  • Simplification of User Interface and Processes to lower the entry point for efficient customer service (provide by CSR or directly by customers).
