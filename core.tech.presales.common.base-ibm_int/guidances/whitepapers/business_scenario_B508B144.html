Main Description

This whitepaper includes a template for describing a business scenario. The typical purpose is to document business activities associated with a business component or process that an organization conducts within an industry or client imperative.  Scenarios reflect the fundamental activities a business performs. Business Scenarios are an element of IBM’s Business Blueprint.


A business scenario would typically be used by a Technical Solution Architect and the client team supporting a Team Solution Design engagement as a basis for developing client recommendations. It may be especially helpful when developing a Client Imperative and/or Transformation Roadmap.

This document includes the following elements.

  • Business Scenario Description
  • Business Outcomes, Benefits and KPI’'s
  • Business or External Capabilities
  • Technical or Supporting Capabilities
  • Business Scenario Scope and Context
  • Actors
  • Business Scenario Use Case
  • Information Sources
  • Business Scenario Maturity Assessment (optional)
  • Conceptual Architecture (optional)
