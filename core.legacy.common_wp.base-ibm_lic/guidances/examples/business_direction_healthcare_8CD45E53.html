Table 2 This table shows how the health system translates its mission, vision, and values into clear goals incorporating the activities of partners.

Goals: In partnership with the community and other providers, we will provide an educational environment that creates and applies new knowledge in all aspects of health care by continuously:

1
Improving clinical outcomes and service.

2
Supporting the University’s Health Science academic mission.

3
Improving customer satisfaction in all groups.

4
Creating a safe and healthful workplace.

5
Enhancing our financial strength and competitiveness.

6
Participating with local and state partners to create healthy communities.



Table 3 This table shows how the health system translates its goals into objectives to which all departments (and employees) can relate.

Objectives (updated every 18 months):

1.1
Support our driving value of “putting patients and families first,” by enhancing clinical outcomes to improve quality and cost effectiveness by redesigning the way care is delivered across the inpatient and ambulatory care continuum.

1.2
Enhance the timeliness, quality, and cost effectiveness of services provided by all Hospitals and Health Centers’ departments and clinical programs.

2.1
Strengthen linkages with the University’s health science schools and programs in order to effectively support their service learning goals and objectives by effectively facilitating their education and research needs.

2.2
Ensure that the Ambulatory Care network operates efficiently, is appropriately sized, and supportive of the health science schools’ academic mission and the delivery of care across the inpatient and outpatient continuum.

3.1
Consistently demonstrate positive customer service behaviors towards patients and families, referring physicians, and each other.

3.2
Work toward a more constructive culture by enhancing employee participation in decision-making, problem-solving, and quality improvement activities.

4.1
Demonstrate a commitment to increasing workplace safety by reducing work related injuries/illnesses.

5.1
Process changes in a timely manner and include necessary and accurate documentation in order to obtain appropriate revenue and reduce write-offs.

5.2
Review and analyze existing and new programs/services to ensure accessibility, efficient utilization of resources, reduce duplication, and increase revenue.

6.1
Actively participate in County Health Improvement Plan (HIP) to:
§ Reduce low birth weight/infant mortality rate in African-Americans;
§ Reduce incidents of abuse and neglect;
§ Increase access to medical care;
§ Increase immunization rates for children under three years of age.

6.2
Establish service to the community as a guiding principle of the institution.
