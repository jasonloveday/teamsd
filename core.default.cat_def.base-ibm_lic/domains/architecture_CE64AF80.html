Domain: Architecture
The work products in the Architecture domain focus on the key technical decisions, including significant elements, patterns and scenarios that drive and constrain the design and overall implementation. They also assist in the resolution of competing concerns and decisions that may be made regarding system qualities.
Main Description
These work products address architecture at the enterprise and solution level.  The work products at the enterprise level provide guidance to promote consistency across multiple projects endeavoring to solve a specific business problem, while the work products at the solution level describe how to capture the architecture of a system intended to satisfy some particular purpose.
