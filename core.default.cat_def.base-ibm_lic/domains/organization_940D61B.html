Domain: Organization
The work products in the Organization domain focus on organizational (people) and cultural aspects of a solution. The work products identify the organization's structure and key areas of impacts and issues that may be affected by the solution.
Main Description
Additionally these work products include information related to the overall transformation and enablement of an organization.
