The Unified Method Framework is the overall brand used to describe the set of Delivery Processes that have been combined under a single architecture, built off of shared reusable components utilizing a common set of standards. The Framework is for use in Solution Design and Solution Delivery.

This is a Solution Design process for Technical Sales at IBM, used by all IBM brands and designed as part of UMF to interlock with delivery processes.
Main Description

Welcome to the Team Solution Design Delivery Process

The audience for Team Solution Design (TeamSD) is IT Architects and Specialists engaged in pre-sales solution design roles. Team Solution Design is an architecture method that also provides guidance in planning and implementation activities for the pre-sales solution designer.

This is version 2.3 of Team Solution Design and the third iteration for the Modernization of the method to reflect IBM’s transformation and the changes in the markets we serve. The continuing evolution of TeamSD is focused on relevancy and provides prescriptive guidance in the use of the method while leveraging leading practices such as IBM Design Thinking and Agile, assets and accelerators. We need to engage with our clients effectively to understand their pain points and challenges so that we can design innovative solutions rapidly, leveraging IBM's strategic platforms / solutions and open standards where appropriate, to deliver meaningful outcomes.

TeamSD along with the companion guidance module for TeamSD in Solution Seeker are referred to as TeamSD Next.

The TeamSD Next Guidance module in Solution Seeker provides the ability to capture notes and early sketches on a Solution Design at an Activity and Task level. This tool provides a checklist interface to make the Activities and Tasks of TeamSD more readily referenced in daily practice. There are links to the full TeamSD method content provided, but the emphasis is on effective practice and early elaboration of work products during Technical Sales. Collaboration features and export of all Opportunity notes and attached images are more recent features added to the TeamSD Next tool.

This third iteration of the TeamSD Next method includes:

  • Changes to how Activities are documented: Adoption of Agile Method Framework’s approach in documenting the content - Each activity has a primary purpose to accomplish and this is manifested in a table of Objectives, Guidance Materials and Tips/Considerations.
  • Addition of guidance:
    • Discovery MindMap that contains the type of information to be gathered or understood to better understand client needs.
    • Developing a Customer Point of View to provide a compelling business-oriented recommendation for customers regarding a specific business problem. The PoV is expected to be a discussion starter and a door opener to pursue further discovery and validation activities with customers.
    • Conducting a Customer Workshop in collaboration with clients to address the impact of business and technology shifts to their business and showing how we can help.
    • Solution Design Recipes which are scenario-specific prescriptive guidance with outcomes, elements or “ingredients” and steps on what to do.
    • Effective use of IBM Design Thinking in support of Solution Design via an updated guideline and new companion whitepaper.
  • Updates to Guidelines and Whitepapers which will be made available in the Integrated Cognitive Solution Design community in the Method Supplemental Guidance Wiki. This reorganization allows for more frequent updates to ensure currency of the guidelines and whitepapers.

The Team Solution Design community is located within the Integrated Cognitive Solution community and is your starting point for information about Team Solution Design including "What's New?", links to education, where to get assistance on Solution Design questions, and much more.

Team Solution Design benefits from the input of the TeamSD board of advisors and input from the over 100 active TeamSD instructors worldwide who continue to make TeamSD a vital method for IBM Technical Leaders across all IBM business units.

Team Solution Design was created to align with Client Value Method (CVM) for IBM Sales and the direction of IBM’s executive leadership (Integration and Value Team: Client Value Initiative) for a unified approach to Solution Design for all IBM business units, with a method defined in the Unified Method Framework. Team Solution Design is that Solution Design process.

Team Solution Design is guided by the following Core Principles:

  1. A single method, across all IBM brands, for pre-sale Solution Design, aligned with the IBM sales method (CVM).
  2. An approach that is part of UMF to provide linkage to the full Solution Development Life Cycle (SDLC)
  3. Demonstrate to clients IBM’s technical leadership in Solution Design and IT Architecture
  4. Support the timely Preparation for Client discussions that is required in pre-sales
  5. Enable Agility in the development of Value Propositions for Clients

The philosophy of Team Solution Design is to provide a pre-integrated, flexible process that can scale (up or down) depending on the size and complexity of the opportunity and the required solution design. TeamSD strongly encourages the use of Assets and the use of Iteration to elaborate the work products to the appropriate level to support both the commitments to clients in our proposals and the transition to successful delivery of client outcomes.

It is important to remember that TeamSD provides a structure for discovery, invention, innovation, insight, analysis and synthesis in support of our client objectives. It is not just about work products! The work products are simply a way to make sure that insights, innovations and conclusions have been captured, for communication as well as for knowledge transfer.
