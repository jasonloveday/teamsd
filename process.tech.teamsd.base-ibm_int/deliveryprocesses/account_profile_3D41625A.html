Work Product (Artifact): Account Profile (ART 0617)
This artifact is an overview of the most current and pertinent account information for a client.
Purpose

It is used internally to prepare for all account planning and reviews, supporting team collaboration and decision-making as follows:

  • Provides a means to update the team on changes to the account in key areas
  • Currency of data in the profile provides a firm view of the client when planning for all engagements with the account
  • Represents the client's agenda in client team decisions-making and execution
  • Used to validate with the client the mission-critical initiatives that will be used as the foundation for the account plan

This artifact is intended to be used internally and with IBM Business Partners to provide updated reference information that will be used when planning client calls and solution options with the client. The Profile provides the latest information about the account (including client business initiatives) for the team to prepare for Team IBM Account Planning and to monitor and track key solution and channel activity of the client. Additionally the profile documents spending patterns with IBM and the competition, and provides account level client preferences as high level context for design and development of new solutions to be proposed and introduced into the client environment.

Description
Main Description

This artifact provides an at-a-glance view of identification details about an account including spending patterns, performance in their industry, business strategy and mission-critical business initiatives, utilization of business partners, client satisfaction and buying preferences.

Key Considerations

Verify with the client relationship team lead that the profile has been updated and that the initiatives reflect the client's current strategy before incorporating the profile information into any preparation for account planning or client calls.

This artifact should be made available to any team member assigned to the account.

Tailoring
Impact of not havingWithout this artifact, important details about the client would not be available to the team to support their account planning efforts.  This lack of insight could result in an invalid strategy for the account and faulty execution tactics.
Reasons for not needingThis artifact is always needed.
