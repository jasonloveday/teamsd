Task: Evaluate Success
This task will evaluate the success of the solution implementation.
Disciplines: Value Confirmation
Purpose

Validate that the project delivered the expect Client Value and met the expectations of the sponsor and other stakeholders and that further projects will be entertained.

As the pre-sale Technical Solution Architect, this is an opportunity to do a self-assessment of the success of your solution design as implemented.

    Main Description

    It is during this task that the actual survey of client satisfaction with the delivered value takes place.

    The Client Value Realization Conversation is designed to capture the client view of the business value that IBM is delivering and to manage the relationship. The Client Value Realization Conversation is conducted in the CVM Phase Confirm of key engagements and on a regular basis with key clients. The output is used to guide future client work. The user guide and template for Client Value Realization Conversation are available at the CVM work products page.

    The objective is to discuss client expectations of value and whether the solution delivered is being used as intended. The two central questions asked regarding the value delivered are:

    • Will you recommend IBM to other clients?
    • Will you choose IBM as a partner again

    If there are any issues that cause the client to be less than fully satisfied, these issues should be documented and an action plan defined to resolve them. The plan might take this form:

    Key Considerations

    Like other tasks in the Team Solution Design Activity: CONFIRM Client Value and Experience, this Task is led by others in IBM and the client. The role of the Technical Solution Architect is to ensure promises made to the client were fulfilled and resulted in the value expected.  If any actions are required to resolve issues, it is important for the solution designer to have at least an advisory role in the resolution.

    The results from this task should be available in the Client Collaboration Hub, serving the dedicated and extended IBM team serving this client. As the Technical Solution Architect, you should also carefully consider this input as you update the Technical Account Plan.

    If there is a teamroom or community used to store client information, the results of this task will be stored there for use on later projects.

    Client Experience Surveys provide additional important insight from your Client. The surveys were designed from the client's perspective in order to provide IBM valuable insight into how well we are creating solutions that support and deliver on the client's business objectives.
