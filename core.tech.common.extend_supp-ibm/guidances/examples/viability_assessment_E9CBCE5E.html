Main Description

1. Approach

A viability assessment was performed for the "Lifestyles is US" home shopping project between 1st and 4th April 2007 at the client's premises. The assessment was performed by doing a series of interviews with project team and by reviewing documentation provided prior to and during the assessment. People interviewed during the assessment were:

  • Steve Scarlet – Chief Architect
  • Beryl Black – Project Manager
  • Graham Green – Senior Java Developer
  • Betty Blue – Business Analyst

The following project documents were provided:

  • LiUS Home Shopping - Architecture Overview, V2.0, 2nd February 2007
  • LiUS Home Shopping – Use Case Model, V3.0, 22nd February 2007
  • LiUS Home Shopping – Component Model, V1.0, 20th March 2007
  • LiUS Home Shopping –Operational Model, V1.0, 20th March 2007


This assessment was carried out at the end of the Solution Outline phase of the project.
