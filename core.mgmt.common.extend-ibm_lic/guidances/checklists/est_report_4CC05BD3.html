Checklist: Estimation Report
The Estimation Results artifact can be validated by following best practices for estimating.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Estimation Report
Check Items
Items being dealt with have be quantified
Verify that the size, schedule and cost documented are quantified.
More than one estimating method/technique has been used
Verify that multiple approachs are documented.
Estimates have been be carried out by a people with experience in the relevant subject area
Verify that the list of people involved is documented.
All assumptions have been documented
Verify that the assumptions are documented for each estimate.
Estimates have been reviewed by a second party

Have an estimating subject matter expert review the content of the report for clarity and accuracy of calculations.

Have an estimating subject matter expert perform a sanity check on the report (e.g. ensure that the project estimate includes all the effort from the technical estimate).
