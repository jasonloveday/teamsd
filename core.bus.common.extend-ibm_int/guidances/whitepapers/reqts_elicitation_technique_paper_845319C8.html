Whitepaper: Requirements Elicitation Technique Paper
The objectives of this technique paper are to:

Focus on techniques and best practices for eliciting business needs, business requirements, and business constraints from the client within the context of producing an IBM bid or proposal to the client. The end result is to have a simple and complete set of business requirements that serve as the foundation for solution design and development.

Identify techniques that the target audience can use immediately in soliciting requirements from the client.
