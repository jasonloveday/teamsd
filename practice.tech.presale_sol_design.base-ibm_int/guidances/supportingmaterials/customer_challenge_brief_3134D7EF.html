Main Description

The Customer Challenge Brief is intended for use by the IBM IT Architects and other client team members to capture information about customer challenges that are expected to be a recurring challenge for many customers. The Customer Challenge Briefs can then be use to accelerate solution identification when that same, or similar, challenge is identified at subsequent customers. The completed Customer Challenge Brief may contain information that is sensitive and proprietary and therefore IBM Confidential. The brief cannot be given to customers, competitors, press, business partners or otherwise distributed. The Customer Challenge Brief template including guidance is an intellectual asset and should be protected and not shared with customers or competitors. Examples of completed challenge briefs for selected customer challenges may be found in iRam. After using the iRam link, it will be necessary to log on to iRam to see the results of the query contained in the link. 

Note: The Customer Challenge Brief is a superset of information contained in the Client Imperative (ART 0696) UMF artifact. The Customer Challenge Brief is intended to document highly pervasive challenges for reuse across many clients. The Client Imperative UMF artifact is intended for use in a specific client situation when developing a transformation roadmap.
