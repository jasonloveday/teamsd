Term Definition: nonconformity
Main Description

An aspect of a deliverable that does not meet one or more acceptance criteria.

In contrast to defect, a non-conformity can be viewed as a failure to meet certain requirements or specifications.

Note: If an aspect of a deliverable is ambiguous with regard to conformity or lack thereof, it is called a controversial defect.
