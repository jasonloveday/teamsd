Term Definition: project
Main Description

WWPMM supports both of the following common and equivalent definitions of a project:

  1. A temporary endeavor undertaken to produce a unique product or service, within a specified scope.
    Operations (such as manufacturing) and projects differ primarily in that operations are ongoing and repetitive, while projects are temporary and unique. (PMI)
  2. A unique process, consisting of a set of coordinated and controlled activities with start and finish dates, undertaken to achieve an objective conforming to specific requirements, including the constraints of time, cost, and resources. (ISO10006)
